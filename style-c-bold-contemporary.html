Our Services

Comprehensive D/deaf
Student Support

From BSL interpreting to deaf awareness training, we provide the specialist services your students need to thrive in education.

BSL Interpreters

Professional BSL/English interpreters who relay between deaf British Sign Language users and hearing people, ensuring full access to lectures, seminars, and tutorials.

Enquire now

Specialist Support Professionals

One-to-one specialist tutoring, result interpretation, enriched language input, and subject-specific support tailored to each student's individual needs.

Enquire now

Specialist Note Taking

Comprehensive, non-verbatim lecture records delivered live via specialist software, or through professional typed and handwritten notes in your preferred format.

Enquire now

Deaf Awareness Training

One-day courses for university staff covering D/deaf culture, history, communication methods, and foundational sign language skills to boost confidence and understanding.

Enquire now

Advocacy & Information

Comprehensive guidance on available support options, from initial enquiry through every stage of student life, ensuring deaf students understand all assistance available to them.

Enquire now

Event Interpreting

Professional BSL interpreters for university events, conferences, graduation ceremonies, open days, and ambassador training sessions.
